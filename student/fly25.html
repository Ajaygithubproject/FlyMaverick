Fly Before 25 Program

A Journey You’ll Remember Forever

The Fly Maverick “Fly Before 25” Program is an emotional movement created for young dreamers who want to explore the world before turning 25. This initiative celebrates fearless travelers who believe that life is meant to be experienced early, boldly, and globally.

More than just travel, this program builds identity and community. It encourages students and young explorers to create unforgettable memories, unlock confidence, and step into the world with a global mindset — before responsibilities take over.

Exclusive Under-25 Benefits

  • Official Fly Before 25 travel badge
  • Special recognition for young travelers
  • Community of global explorers

Free Perks Included

  • Personalized travel certificate
  • Social media feature on Fly Maverick
  • Exclusive traveler community access
  • Priority invites for youth travel events

Perfect For: College students, first-time travelers, backpackers, and young explorers under 25.
